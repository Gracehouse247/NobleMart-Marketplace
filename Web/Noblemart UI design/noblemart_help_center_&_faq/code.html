Top Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common queries

How to track my order? expand_more
You can track your order by clicking on the 'Track Order' link in the top menu or through your account dashboard under 'My Orders'. You'll receive real-time updates as your package moves from our warehouse to your doorstep.
What is Escrow payment? expand_more
Escrow payment is NobleMart's secure payment system where we hold your funds until you confirm receipt of your item in the described condition. This ensures both buyers and sellers are protected in every transaction.
How do I return a faulty item? expand_more
NobleMart offers a 7-day return policy for faulty items. Simply navigate to 'My Orders', select the item you wish to return, and click 'Initiate Return'. Our logistics partner will schedule a pickup within 48 hours.
What are the shipping fees? expand_more
Shipping fees vary based on your location and the weight of the items. You can view the exact shipping cost on the checkout page before completing your order. Free shipping is available on select items for NobleMart Plus members.
