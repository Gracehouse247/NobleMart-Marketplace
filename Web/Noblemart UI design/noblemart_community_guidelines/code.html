Detailed Marketplace Rules

person Buyer Conduct & Responsibilities expand_more

Buyers are expected to complete payments for all confirmed orders. Harassment of vendors or misuse of the rating system to extort discounts is strictly prohibited.

  • Provide accurate delivery information.
  • Respond to vendor queries regarding order specifications.
  • Report issues through official channels only.
storefront Vendor Standards & Performance expand_more

Vendors must maintain a minimum fulfillment rate of 95%. Listings must be accurate, including clear photos and detailed descriptions of any defects or variations.

  • Ship orders within the promised 48-hour window.
  • Honoring warranty and return policies.
  • Maintain professional customer service standards.
payments Escrow & Payments Safety expand_more

All payments must be processed through NoblePay Escrow. Attempts to bypass our payment system ("Off-platform trading") will result in immediate permanent suspension of all involved parties.

gavel Anti-Counterfeit Policy expand_more

NobleMart enforces a strict zero-tolerance policy for fake or knock-off goods. Vendors found listing counterfeit items will have their stores closed and funds withheld pending legal review.

Enforcement: The NobleShield System

To ensure a world-class experience, we utilize a transparent tier-based penalty system for any guideline violations.

01

Warning & Remediation

First-time minor offenses receive a formal warning and required policy retraining.

02

Temporary Suspension

Repeat minor offenses or major first-time violations result in a 7-30 day suspension.

03

Permanent Ban

Severe violations including fraud or counterfeit sales lead to immediate and permanent expulsion.

Mediation & Appeals

Dissatisfied with a decision? Our independent mediation team reviews all appeals within 48 business hours. We believe in fair hearing for all entrepreneurs.
