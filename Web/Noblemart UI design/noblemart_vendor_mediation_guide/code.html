4-Step Mediation Roadmap

The lifecycle of a dispute on NobleMart

1. Dispute Notification

Receive an alert via the Seller Dashboard or Email. Review the buyer's reason for the dispute immediately.

Best Practices

Do not take it personally. Respond professionally to show your commitment to quality.

notification_important
description

2. Evidence Collection

The "Golden Hour": Gather photos, shipping receipts, and conversation logs. You have 48 hours to upload proof.

Best Practices

Ensure all photos have clear timestamps and show the item condition before packing.

3. Mediator Review

A NobleMart specialist reviews both claims based on platform policy and the evidence provided.

Best Practices

Check your inbox regularly in case the mediator requests additional clarification.

4. Final Settlement

Escrow funds are released to the winning party. If a partial refund is agreed, the split is handled instantly.

Best Practices

Close the loop by asking the mediator for feedback to avoid future disputes.
